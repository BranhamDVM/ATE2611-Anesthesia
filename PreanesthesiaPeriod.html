1a. Identifying Preanesthetic Exam Equipment +

A thorough physical exam requires specific tools. Know them!

  • **Stethoscope:** For auscultation of the heart and lungs.
  • **Thermometer:** To accurately assess core body temperature (often rectal).
  • **Otoscope/Ophthalmoscope:** For detailed exam of ears/eyes (when indicated).
  • **Reflex Hammer:** To check neurological reflexes.
  • **Scale:** To obtain an accurate and current **patient weight** (crucial for drug dosing!).
  • **Watch/Timer with Second Hand:** For accurate measurement of heart rate, respiratory rate, and pulse quality.
